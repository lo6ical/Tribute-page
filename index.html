Elon Reeve Musk

Elon Reeve Musk (born June 28, 1971, Pretoria, South Africa) is a South African-born American entrepreneur who cofounded electronic-payment firm PayPal and formed SpaceX. He was also one of the first significant investors in, as well as chief executive officer of the electric car manufacturer Tesla.

Early Life

Musk was born to a South African father and a Canadian mother. He displayed an early talent for computers ans entrepreneurship. At age 12 he created a video game and sold it to a computer magazine. In 1988, after obtaining a Canadian passport, Musk left South Africa because he was unwilling to support apartheid through compulsory military service and because he sought the greater economic opportunities available in the United States.
